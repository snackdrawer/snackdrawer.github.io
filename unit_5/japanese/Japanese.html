Group classes

Group class are held at our office at 315 Madison Avenue. Class are small Maximum 8 for beginners and 6 for upper levels, minimum 3 students.


Private

With this basic framework, each teacher creates and develops her own teaching materials based on student needs and goals.


Online

All our online private lessons are held in one of our online classrooms. Please when contacting us let us know your availability and preferences for times and days for lessons.


Intensive programs

Designed to immerse you in the language, we recommend lessons at least once a day, and lessons are usually held at our center where we use multimedia in the classroom to enhance the immersion experience.
