ABOUT HILLS LEARNING

Hills Learning was founded in January 2009 and is a family-owned business. We currently have over 20 teachers and a variety of programs for over 200 students. Our clients include fortune 500 companies, schools (private and universities), and happy individuals throughout the New York City area.

We inspire the confidence to communicate in a foreign language, stimulate motivation to achieve each individual's goals, and encourage a lifetime of language learning. We do this by putting students first. We prioritize student goals and needs in our everyday work.

A good teacher is our best asset. We spend lots of time and energy recruiting some of the best teachers for the languages we offer. The school also invests in our teacher's growth through our training programs.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Hills Learning philosophy on language learning is you learn language from people and through interaction. "Highly Interactive Language Learning" means following our SWIRL teaching method in our classes will enable students to achieve their language goals.
